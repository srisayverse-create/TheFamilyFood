About TheFamilyFood

Our Story

1985 mein Sharma family ne ek chhota sa vegetarian restaurant shuru kiya tha ek simple sapne ke saath - families ko authentic, ghar jaisa khana dena aur unhe ek saath laana.

Jo baat nani ke kitchen mein shuru hui thi, aaj ek famous restaurant chain ban gayi hai. Lekin humne apni jaadein kabhi nahi bhooli. Har recipe abhi bhi traditional family formulas par based hai jo generations se chali aa rahi hai.

Aaj hum hazaaron families ko serve karte hain, lekin quality, warmth, aur authentic vegetarian flavors ke liye wahi commitment hai jo shuru mein thi. Pure vegetarian, pure swad!
